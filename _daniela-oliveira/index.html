About me

I’m Daniela Oliveira, a designer interested in UX/UI and digital design based in Lisbon, Portugal.

Been working in advertising & social media strategy for the past two years coming up with creative concepts to help brands tell stories.

As a 360° project kind of professional, I’ve been working with video, photography, graphic and motion design since 2015.

I’m also very curious about programming and web development. Spent a year learning Python and been trying to understand how technology works on the back-end.

+ + + Three facts about me!

I eat too much, rollerskate and shred skateparks for more time than my schedule can handle & I’m always down to travel around (but only if I can bring my skates with me).
